BIOGRAPHY

 

A biography is the narration of a person’s life by someone else.

 

Biographies can be as short as a paragraph or as long as a novel. But all biographies have something in

 

common, they are divided into the early life of the person, how they started in their career and the legacy

 

they left. Read the biographies in this unit: Dolores del Río on page 80; Leonardo Da Vinci on page 84,

 

Sandra Cisneros on page 88 and Octavio Paz on page 96.

 

Write down all the things they have in common and the differences.
